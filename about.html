About

The Congenial 33 Room is the taproom at The Old Pabst Brewery in Peoria Heights, Illinois. We welcome you and yours for private rentals, popup events, intimate performances and more.

Our goal is to create a warm, inviting space for private gatherings, community moments, and memorable evenings in the heart of Peoria Heights.

For reservations, events, or questions, please reach out at the33room@gmail.com or explore the links above.
