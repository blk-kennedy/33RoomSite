# Host an Event at The 33 Room
![Reserve The Room](/assets/images/press.jpg)

Warm, midcentury modern finishes decorate our welcoming and open events space, which can host up to 45 people. We’ll work with you to get furniture set up how you’d like it—private dinners, receptions, meetings, performances.

Host an event in the historic hospitality suite of the former Pabst Brewery with a full-service bar, curated menu offerings, and on-site hospitality.

Maximum capacity: 49. There is a 20-guest minimum to rent the room. The 33 Room is not available for rent after 5pm on Fridays and Saturdays. Renters are responsible for damage or excessive cleaning fees.

To inquire or reserve, email: [the33room@gmail.com](mailto:the33room@gmail.com?subject=Reserve%20The%20%2233%22%20Room&body=I%20would%20like%20to%20reserve%20the%2033%20Room%20for...)


## Cancellations & Refunds

Cancellations are accepted 30 days prior to the reservation. Partial refunds may be available within 30 days on a case-by-case basis.
