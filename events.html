Happenings at The 33 Room

We think of The 33 Room as the kind of lounge a friend would open. Hospitality is about creating a personal experience: intimate concerts, VIP events, spoken word, performance art, drawing jams, book clubs, and writer's evenings are all welcome.

The space invites an understated urban vibe with a hip, vintage ambiance true to the room's aesthetic. Reserve the room to host your own event or follow us on socials to see what's happening next.
